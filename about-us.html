About Us

MiTRA Demo Logistics Pvt Ltd.

MiTRA Demo Logistics Private Limited is one of the largest integrated supply chain management (SCM) solutions providers in India. We provide one-stop SCM solutions at our facility in Taloja including warehousing (bonded, general), clearing & forwarding, container freight station and transportation services. Our dedicated team of warehousing, clearing & forwarding and transport professionals serve a wide range of businesses from coffeehouse companies to manufacturing and infrastructure companies.

Our Facilities

  • Container Freight Station at Taloja (JNPT area) with 60,000 sq.ft. covered warehouse for export activities, 1,25,000 sq.ft. of covered warehouse for bonded import activities and open yard for handling 3,000 TEUs per month
  • Clearing and forwarding
  • Experienced team in customs formalities, materials handling and transport
  • 24 x 7 operations
  • Adjacent to the CFS, on contiguous land, we have over 1,40,000 sq.ft. of bonded covered warehousing area, over 1,25,000 sq.ft. of general covered warehousing area and more than 3,00,000 sq.ft. of bonded and general open warehousing area.
  • Reefer points
  • Round the clock security coverage with security guards and CCTV surveillance
  • Temperature controlled warehousing will be provided on request
